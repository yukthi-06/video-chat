# Delayed Disconnect on Upload Completion Plan

Implement a graceful shutdown mechanism where disconnecting from the call is delayed until all outstanding video files (both outgoing uploads for non-admins and incoming downloads for admins) are fully completed.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> - Recorders will finalize their current 60-second segment immediately upon call termination.
> - The application will keep the WebSocket connection open and show a "Waiting for uploads..." message, only closing the connection and finishing the activity once all transfers are complete.
