# User Request: Recording should start only after other participant starts video

## Goal
To record two MP4 files per call (local stream and remote stream) but defer the initiation of both encoders and muxers until the connection with the remote peer is fully established and their video track is active.

## Solution Details
1. **Defer Recorder Activation**:
   - Modified [CallActivity.java](file:///x:/Projects_X/0_Active/8_Android_APK/04_Early_Stage/Video_Chat_APK_GITHUB/app/src/main/java/com/example/videochatapp/activities/CallActivity.java) to instantiate the local/remote video recorders inside `initWebRtcAndSignaling()`, but removed the immediate calls to `localRecorder.start()` and `remoteRecorder.start()`.
2. **Trigger Recording on Peer Video Received**:
   - Updated `onRemoteTrackAdded(VideoTrack track)` in [CallActivity.java](file:///x:/Projects_X/0_Active/8_Android_APK/04_Early_Stage/Video_Chat_APK_GITHUB/app/src/main/java/com/example/videochatapp/activities/CallActivity.java) to call `localRecorder.start()` and `remoteRecorder.start()` and bind the remote recorder as a sink to the remote video track.
   - Showing a "Call connected. Recording started." Toast notification to make it clear that the recording phase has begun.
